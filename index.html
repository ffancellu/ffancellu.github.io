I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Bonnie Webber and Adam Lopez. My work focuses on different problems related to automatically detecting and representing negation for NLP. Few of the research questions that have been bugging me are:

  • Can we build better and simpler negation scope detection system for Information Extraction applications (ACL2016)...
  • ... and what is that these systems learn from the data we feed them? (EACL2017-to appear)
  • Can we improve the performance of MT systems on negation? (EACL2014, Ex-Prom2015)
  • Can we represent negation scope on top of existing, widely-used resources? (ArXiv-to appear)

Previously, I have worked (and enjoyed working!) for CNGL(now ADAPT)@TCD as an NLP research engineer.

I have a soft spot for East Asian Languages I have been studying and researching for a decade now. I am still (occasionally) a Korean-to-Italian translator.
