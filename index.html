I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Edinburgh where I am researching in the field of Statistical Machine Translation under the supervision of Prof. Bonnie Webber and Adam Lopez. My work focuses on building systems for cross-lingual negation detection and their application to Machine Translation

In the year in between the completion of my MSc and the beginning of my Ph.D. I have worked (and enjoyed working!) for CNGL(now ADAPT)@TCD as an NLP research engineer.

I have a soft spot for East Asian Languages I have been studying and researching for a decade now.

I am still (occasionally) a Korean-to-Italian translator.
